**I Dared to Dream.**

It was during my 2nd term (1/12/2018) when I clicked this picture. On that day I promised myself, no matter what I will win this Scholarship, it was the only scholarship available in our campus.

That time I had no idea about the whole procedure, criteria or anything, but I was determined to achieve it.

I was nowhere close to a scholar during my graduation days, in fact I was way behind those backbenches. Even dreaming about this scholarship was a daring task for me.

I worked hard, day in and day out for the entire duration of my course, so that I could become eligible to apply for this scholarship. I had to compete with top performers of the batch who were nominated for the scholarship.

Today, I was awarded the scholarship.

![The OPJEMS award ceremony](/journal/scholarship-ceremony.jpg)

> Believe in yourself. Dare to dream. Dreams do come to life.
