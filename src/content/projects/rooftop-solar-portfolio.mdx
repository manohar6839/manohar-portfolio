## Overview

Led the technical design for rooftop solar installations on three commercial buildings in Gurugram, Haryana. The project involved site surveys, shading analysis, and optimal panel layout design.

## Project Scope

- Site assessment and structural analysis
- Shadow analysis using AutoCAD
- Energy yield simulation in PVsyst
- Inverter and balance of system (BOS) selection
- Grid interconnection design

## Results

- Total capacity: 500 kW
- Estimated annual generation: 750 MWh
- Payback period: 4.5 years
- Carbon offset: 540 tonnes CO2 annually
